UPenn — NEISS Medical Narrative Classification

Aug 2024 – Jun 2025 · Python · LLMs (OpenAI/LLaMA) · Fine‑tuning · Evaluation

I am the lead author of a pipeline to classify injury narratives into standardized body part and injury type using a subset of the NEISS dataset. I built prompt-based baselines, created two accuracy metrics (exact dictionary match, semantic match), and fine‑tuned models to improve accuracy. Presented results at TNJSEF and Rice University's AI in Healthcare.

Aikium AI — Protein Solubility Prediction

Summer 2025 · ESM2 embeddings · FFN · Feature Engineering

I developed a hybrid model combining ESM2 embeddings with physiochemical features and clustering to surpass existing benchmarks in protein solubility prediction. I built reproducible data pipelines and benchmarking harness; saved weights for reuse and evaluation on new datasets. I am presenting my results at MIT Broad Institute's Machine Learning in Drug Discovery Conference.

AlgoVerse — Breast Cancer MRI Survivability

2025 · 3D CNNs · DICOM · Multimodal

I was the team camptain of a project to create models to predict survivability using Duke Cancer Archive MRI (and notes). I implemented DICOM preprocessing, experimented with 3D CNN architectures, and evaluated runs. We are currently preparing for an ISBI 2026 submission.
